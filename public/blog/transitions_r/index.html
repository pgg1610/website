Transition words and phrases R

Transition words/phrases are writing devices which help bridge ideas and guide the reader through the prose. When used strategically and sparingly they connect multiple ideas and make the flow you’d have planned much more clearer to the reader.

In my experience, I have made use of transition words even when delivering talks. They help add in necessary connections as you might glide through your slide deck and the information in the previous slide is not directly available to your audience.

“Music is the space between the notes” - Claude Debussy
